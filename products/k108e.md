K108E is a digitally controlled dual VCDT (Voltage Controlled Digital Traveler). It simulates classic VCF behavior and legendary "traveler" circuits to create unique sonic motion.

## Features

- Dual digitally-controlled traveler circuits
- Classic VCF behavior simulation
- Legendary "traveler" circuit emulation
- Unique sonic motion and filter characteristics
- CV control over all parameters

Digital traveler filter (in development).

## Features

- Resonant lowpass filter
- Traveler-style modulation
