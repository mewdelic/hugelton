A level meter with 9-segment Neopixel LEDs. AC/DC switching and peak hold functions allow intuitive visualization of CV and audio signal states.

## Features

- 9-segment Neopixel LED display
- AC/DC switching
- Peak hold function
- Visualizes CV and audio signals
