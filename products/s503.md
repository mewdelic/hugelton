A simple yet experimental rhythm computer. Combining step input with algorithms and probabilistic variations to generate unpredictable rhythmic patterns. Create new grooves when combined with drum modules and trigger inputs.

## Features

- Step sequencer with algorithmic variations
- Probabilistic pattern generation
- Trigger output for drum modules
- Experimental rhythm creation
