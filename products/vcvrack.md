The K-Series modules for VCV Rack bring the unique sound and functionality of Hügelton's hardware synthesizers to the virtual modular environment. Each module faithfully recreates the characteristics of its hardware counterpart while taking advantage of VCV Rack's flexible patching capabilities.

## Modules

- **K102V** - Dual Oscillator
- **K104V** - Envelope Generator
- Faithful recreation of hardware characteristics
- Flexible VCV Rack patching
